               Welcome to ' Kids football shootout ' !
 
This game consist of three levels:
    - in level 1 the footballer have 5 tries if he score 3 or more , he move to level 2.
 
    - in level 2 the footballer have 7 tries if he score 4 or more , he move to level 3.
 
    - in level 3 the footballer have 10 tries .

 
Scoring rules:
    Footballer:
        1) +50 if score a goal.
        2)  -15  If his ball is hit with distraction balls.
 	
    Goalkeeper:
        1) +50 if catch a ball.
        2) +35 if footballer timeout has expired.
